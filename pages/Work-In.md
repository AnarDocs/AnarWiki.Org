**Work-Ins** refer to a method of
"<a   href="strike.md" class="wikilink" title="strike">"strike</a> undertaken
in the "<a   href="United_Kingdom.md" class="wikilink" title="UK">"UK</a>,
"<a   href="Canada.md" class="wikilink" title="Canada">"Canada</a> and
"<a   href="Australia.md" class="wikilink" title="Australia">"Australia</a>
throughout the 1970s and early 1980s.
"<a   href="Workers&#39;_Self-Management.md" class="wikilink"
title="Workers&#39; self-management">"Workers' self-management</a> is
practiced in the workplace in protest of some action by an
"<a   href="Boss.md" class="wikilink" title="employer or manager">"employer or
manager</a>.
