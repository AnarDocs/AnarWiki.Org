**Work-Ins** refer to a method of
<a href="strike" class="wikilink" title="strike">strike</a> undertaken
in the <a href="United_Kingdom" class="wikilink" title="UK">UK</a>,
<a href="Canada" class="wikilink" title="Canada">Canada</a> and
<a href="Australia" class="wikilink" title="Australia">Australia</a>
throughout the 1970s and early 1980s.
<a href="Workers&#39;_Self-Management" class="wikilink"
title="Workers&#39; self-management">Workers' self-management</a> is
practiced in the workplace in protest of some action by an
<a href="Boss" class="wikilink" title="employer or manager">employer or
manager</a>.
