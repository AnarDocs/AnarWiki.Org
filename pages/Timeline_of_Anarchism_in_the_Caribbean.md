![](Carribean.jpg "Carribean.jpg") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in Caribbean that are relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.

- 1892: <a href="University_of_Havana_Strike_(1892)" class="wikilink"
  title="University of Havana Strike">University of Havana Strike</a>
  (Cuba)
- 1903:
  <a href="Trinidad_and_Tobago_Water_Riots_(1903)" class="wikilink"
  title="Trinidad and Tobago Water Riots">Trinidad and Tobago Water
  Riots</a>
- 1953: <a href="Cuban_Revolution" class="wikilink"
  title="Cuban Revolution">Cuban Revolution</a>
- 1994: <a href="Maleconazo_(1994)" class="wikilink"
  title="Maleconazo">Maleconazo</a> (Cuba)
- 2006: <a href="Aruba_Oilworkers_Strike_(2006)" class="wikilink"
  title="Aruba Oilworkers&#39; Strike">Aruba Oilworkers' Strike</a>
  (Caribbean Netherlands)
