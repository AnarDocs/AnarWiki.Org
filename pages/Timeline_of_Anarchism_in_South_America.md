![](SouthAmerica.jpg "SouthAmerica.jpg") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in South America (a region encompassing the states of
<a href="Brazil" class="wikilink" title="Brazil">Brazil</a>,
<a href="Colombia" class="wikilink" title="Colombia">Colombia</a>,
<a href="Argentina" class="wikilink" title="Argentina">Argentina</a>,
<a href="Venezuela" class="wikilink" title="Venezuela">Venezuela</a>,
<a href="Peru" class="wikilink" title="Peru">Peru</a>,
<a href="Chile" class="wikilink" title="Chile">Chile</a>,
<a href="Ecuador" class="wikilink" title="Ecuador">Ecuador</a>,
<a href="Bolivia" class="wikilink" title="Bolivia">Bolivia</a>,
<a href="Paraguay" class="wikilink" title="Paraguay">Paraguay</a>,
<a href="Uruguay" class="wikilink" title="Uruguay">Uruguay</a>,
<a href="Guyana" class="wikilink" title="Guyana">Guyana</a>,
<a href="Suriname" class="wikilink" title="Suriname">Suriname</a> and
<a href="French_Guiana" class="wikilink" title="French Guiana">French
Guiana</a>) that are relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.
