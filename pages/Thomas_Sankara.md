## Life

### Assassination

In a coup supported by Burkina Faso's ruling class,
"<a   href="France.md" class="wikilink" title="France">France</a>, the
"<a   href="United_States_of_America.md" class="wikilink" title="USA">USA</a>,
"<a   href="Israel.md" class="wikilink" title="Israel">Israel</a>, Libya,
"<a   href="Ivory_Coast.md" class="wikilink" title="Ivory Coast">Ivory
Coast</a>, "<a   href="Togo.md" class="wikilink" title="Togo">Togo</a> and
"<a   href="Liberia.md" class="wikilink" title="Liberia">Liberia</a>, Sankara
was killed in a gunfight, his body dismembered and thrown into an
unmarked grave.
