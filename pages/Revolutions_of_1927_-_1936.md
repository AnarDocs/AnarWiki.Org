The **Revolutions of 1927 - 1936** were a
<a href="Revolutionary_Wave" class="wikilink"
title="revolutionary wave">revolutionary wave</a> consisting off mass
political unrest and revolts around the world in the early 20th century.
The uprisings were mainly socialist or anti-colonial in nature and were
mostly short-lived, failing to have a long-term impact.

## Revolutions

- 1927: Nanchang Uprising
  (<a href="China" class="wikilink" title="China">China</a>)
- 1927: Sandinista Uprising (Nicaragua)
- 1930: <a href="Nghệ-Tĩnh_Revolt_(1930)" class="wikilink"
  title="Nghệ-Tĩnh Revolt">Nghệ-Tĩnh Revolt</a>
  (<a href="Vietnam" class="wikilink" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>)
- 1933: Cuban Revolution
- 1934: <a href="February_Uprising_(Austria)" class="wikilink"
  title="February Uprising">February Uprising</a> (Austria)
- 1934: Asturian Uprising (Spain)
- 1936: Spanish Revolution
