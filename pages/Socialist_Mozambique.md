The **People's Republic of Mozambique**Â was a
<a href="Marxist-Leninism" class="wikilink"
title="Marxist-Leninist">Marxist-Leninist</a> state that existed in
<a href="Mozambique" class="wikilink" title="Mozambique">Mozambique</a>
from 1975 to 1990.

## Positives

- The army had no formal hierarchy and was based on a system of elected
  commanders, making it a <a href="Democratic_Military" class="wikilink"
  title="democratic military">democratic military</a>.
- Corruption was apparently fairly rare and abuses of power (including
  war crimes) were harshly punished.
- It was one of the few Marxist-Leninist governments to openly promote
  <a href="Workers&#39;_Self-Management" class="wikilink"
  title="workers&#39; self-management">workers' self-management</a>.

## Negatives

- The government restricted freedom of the press.
