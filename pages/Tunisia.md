**Tunisia**, officially the **Republic of Tunisia** is a
<a href="Liberalism" class="wikilink" title="liberal">liberal</a>,
<a href="Capitalism" class="wikilink" title="capitalist">capitalist</a>,
<a href="Colonialism" class="wikilink" title="colonial">colonial</a>
<a href="List_of_States" class="wikilink" title="state">state</a>
located in Northern Africa, near
<a href="Algeria" class="wikilink" title="Algeria">Algeria</a>,
<a href="Libya" class="wikilink" title="Libya">Libya</a> and
<a href="Italy" class="wikilink" title="Italy">Italy</a>.

## History

Tunisia was an Ottoman and French colony before being under two
dictatorships and finally democracy.

## Positives

- Tunisia has implemented many feminist policies, including outlawing
  domestic violence, polygamy and easier divorces for men. Interfaith
  marriage and freedom to dress are legal.
