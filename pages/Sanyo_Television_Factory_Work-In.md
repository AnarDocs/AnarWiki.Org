The **Sanyo Television Factory Work-In** was a 10-day
<a href="Work-In.md" class="wikilink" title="work-in">work-in</a> in
Wodonga,
<a href="Australia.md" class="wikilink" title="Australia">Australia</a>.
<a href="Working_Class.md" class="wikilink" title="Workers">Workers</a>
took over the
<a href="factory.md" class="wikilink" title="factory">factory</a>, forcing
<a href="Bosses.md" class="wikilink" title="management">management</a> to
listen to their demands which stopped many workers from being
<a href="Unemployment.md" class="wikilink" title="fired">fired</a> and
increased
<a href="Wage_Labour.md" class="wikilink" title="wages">wages</a>.[^1]
