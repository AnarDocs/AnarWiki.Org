# Ukrainian famine, 1932-1933

There is a section on the left either completely denying famines
occurred or blaming peasants for their own demise, despite Soviet
Archives being open to the public as well as both Russian academia and
Russian state government acknowledging failures of Soviet policies.
Understandably, Moscow (or Kremlin), the rest of Russia (outside of
Moscow) and nations adjacent to Russia will have varying narratives.
