The **International Workers' Association** (**IWA**or **AIT**) is an
<a href="Confederation.md" class="wikilink"
title="international federation">international federation</a> of
<a href="Anarcho-Syndicalism.md" class="wikilink"
title="anarcho-syndicalist">anarcho-syndicalist</a> trade unions and
initiatives.

## Current Members

*Sections of the IWA are openly anarcho-syndicalist organisations and
can vote at congresses. Friends are regarded as fellow travellers and
send observers to congresses.*
