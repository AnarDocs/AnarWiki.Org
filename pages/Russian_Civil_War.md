The **Russian Civil War** took place in the aftermath of the fall of the
"<a   href="Russian_Empire.md" class="wikilink" title="Russian Empire">Russian
Empire</a> and the
"<a   href="February_Revolution_(Russia).md" class="wikilink"
title="two">two</a>
"<a   href="October_Revolution_(Russia).md" class="wikilink"
title="revolutions">revolutions</a> from 1917 to 1922. The war was
largely fought between 'Reds' (composed of the Bolsheviks and their
allies) and the 'Whites' (composed of various anti-Bolshevik groups,
including monarchists, republicans, capitalists and even more moderate
socialists). There were also more independent anarchist groups (like the
"<a   href="Black_Army_(Ukraine).md" class="wikilink" title="Black Army">Black
Army</a>) and 'Green Armies' who defended their towns and land from
exploitation by others.

An estimated 7,000,000 to 12,000,000 were killed as a result of the war
(compared to 15,000,000 to 19,000,000 deaths in
"<a   href="World_War_I.md" class="wikilink" title="World War I">World War
I</a>[^1]). Russia's economy was destroyed, it is estimated that the
total output of mines and factories in 1921 had fallen to 20% of the
pre-World War level, and many crucial items experienced an even more
drastic decline. For example, cotton production fell to 5%, and iron to
2%, of pre-war levels.
