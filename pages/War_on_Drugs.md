The **War on Drugs** is a global campaign led by the
<a href="United_States_of_America" class="wikilink" title="USA">USA</a>
which has the stated purpose of wanting to end the illegal trade and use
of <a href="drugs" class="wikilink" title="drugs">drugs</a>. It began in
1971, and is largely been regarded to be a colossal failure of policy
and even <a href="Conservatism" class="wikilink"
title="conservatives">conservatives</a> have begun calling for its end.
However, some critics have argued that the campaign is a highly
successful cover story involved in disrupting social movements,
increasing the
<a href="Rate_of_Profit" class="wikilink" title="rate of profit">rate of
profit</a> and justifying giving more power to the
<a href="State_(Polity)" class="wikilink" title="state">state</a>.
