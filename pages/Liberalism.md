**Liberalism** is an ideology that generally supports limited
government, individual rights (including civil rights and human rights),
<a href="capitalism.md" class="wikilink" title="capitalism">capitalism</a>
(free markets), private property, representative democracy, secularism,
legal <a href="Gender_Equality.md" class="wikilink"
title="gender equality">gender equality</a>, legal racial equality,
internationalism, freedom of speech, freedom of the press and freedom of
religion.

## History

### Proto-Liberalism

Liberalism has very similar origins to
<a href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>,
both being movements defending the ideals of
<a href="freedom.md" class="wikilink" title="freedom">freedom</a> and
equality.

## Criticism

Liberalism has been criticised by socialists
(<a href="Authoritarian_Socialism.md" class="wikilink"
title="authoritarian">authoritarian</a> and
<a href="Libertarian_Socialism.md" class="wikilink"
title="libertarian">libertarian</a>) for not taking an active stance
against
<a href="capitalism.md" class="wikilink" title="capitalism">capitalism</a>
and it's hypocritical usage of violence. Liberal governments frequently
directly or indirectly conduct massive atrocities against their own
civilians and those of other countries via colonialism and
neo-colonialism.
