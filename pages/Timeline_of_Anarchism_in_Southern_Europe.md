![](SouthernEurope.JPG "SouthernEurope.JPG") This is a timeline of
various political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental
events that have occurred in Southern Europe (an areas including
<a href="Portugal" class="wikilink" title="Portugal">Portugal</a>,
<a href="Spain" class="wikilink" title="Spain">Spain</a>,
<a href="Italy" class="wikilink" title="Italy">Italy</a>,
<a href="Greece" class="wikilink" title="Greece">Greece</a>,
<a href="Slovenia" class="wikilink" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>,
<a href="Bosnia" class="wikilink" title="Bosnia and Herzegovina">Bosnia
and Herzegovina</a>,
<a href="Albania" class="wikilink" title="Albania">Albania</a>, Serbia,
Kosovo, Montenegro, North Macedonia and Croatia) that are relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.
Southern Europe is likely the area of the world where anarchism is the
most popular.
