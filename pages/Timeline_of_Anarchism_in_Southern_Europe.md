![](SouthernEurope.JPG "SouthernEurope.JPG") This is a timeline of
various political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental
events that have occurred in Southern Europe (an areas including
"<a   href="Portugal.md" class="wikilink" title="Portugal">"Portugal</a>,
"<a   href="Spain.md" class="wikilink" title="Spain">"Spain</a>,
"<a   href="Italy.md" class="wikilink" title="Italy">"Italy</a>,
"<a   href="Greece.md" class="wikilink" title="Greece">"Greece</a>,
"<a   href="Slovenia.md" class="wikilink" title="Slovenia">"Slovenia</a>,
"<a   href="Bosnia.md" class="wikilink" title="Bosnia and Herzegovina">"Bosnia
and Herzegovina</a>,
"<a   href="Albania.md" class="wikilink" title="Albania">"Albania</a>, Serbia,
Kosovo, Montenegro, North Macedonia and Croatia) that are relevant to
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">"anarchism</a>.
Southern Europe is likely the area of the world where anarchism is the
most popular.
