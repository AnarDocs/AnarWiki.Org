**Squatting** is the act of occupying an abandoned or unoccupied area of
"<a   href="land.md" class="wikilink" title="land">land</a> or a building,
usually residential, that the **squatter** does not own, rent or
otherwise have lawful permission to use. In 2004, there were an
estimated one billion squatters around the world, mainly in much of the
"<a   href="Global_South.md" class="wikilink" title="Global South">Global
South</a>, where extensive slums and shanty towns built on the edge of
major cities built without permission. In the
"<a   href="Global_North.md" class="wikilink" title="Global North">Global
North</a>, squatting is used by "<a   href="Autonomism.md" class="wikilink"
title="autonomists">autonomists</a> and
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchists">anarchists</a>
to provide free housing and social centers to cities.
