![](SoutheranAfrica.JPG "SoutheranAfrica.JPG") This is a timeline of
various political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental
events that have occurred in Southern Africa (an area that includes the
states of
"<a   href="South_Africa.md" class="wikilink" title="South Africa">South
Africa</a>, Lesotho, eSwatini,
"<a   href="Namibia.md" class="wikilink" title="Namibia">Namibia</a>,

Zimbabwe, Madagascar, Malawi, Mozambique and
"<a   href="Mauritius.md" class="wikilink" title="Mauritius">Mauritius</a>)
that are relevant to
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.
