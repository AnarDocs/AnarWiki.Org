![](WesternAsia.JPG "WesternAsia.JPG") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in Western Asia (an area including the states of
Turkey, Syria,
"<a   href="Israel.md" class="wikilink" title="Israel">Israel</a>, Palestine,
Jordan, Lebanon,
"<a   href="Saudi_Arabia.md" class="wikilink" title="Saudi Arabia">Saudi
Arabia</a>, Yemen, Oman, the UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, Iraq, Iran and
Afghanistan) that are relevant to
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.
