![](EasternEurope.JPG "EasternEurope.JPG") This is a timeline of
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in Eastern Europe that are relevant to
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">"anarchism</a>.

- 1903: "<a   href="Strandzha_Commune.md" class="wikilink"
  title="Strandzha Republic">"Strandzha Republic</a> (now Bulgaria)
- 1918: "<a   href="Free_Territory_of_Ukraine.md" class="wikilink"
  title="Free Territory of Ukraine">"Free Territory of Ukraine</a>
- 1921: "<a   href="Kronstadt_Rebellion.md" class="wikilink"
  title="Kronstadt Rebellion">"Kronstadt Rebellion</a> (Russia)
- 1939: "<a   href="The_Hajduks_of_Cotovschi.md" class="wikilink"
  title="The Hajduks of Cotovschi">"The Hajduks of Cotovschi</a> founded
  (Romania)
- 1968:
  "<a   href="Prague_Spring.md" class="wikilink" title="Prague Spring">"Prague
  Spring</a> (Czechoslovakia)
- 1968: "<a   href="March_Events_in_Poland_(1968).md" class="wikilink"
  title="March Events">"March Events</a> (Poland)
- 2006: "<a   href="Rog.md" class="wikilink" title="Rog">"Rog</a> squatted
  (Slovenia)
- 2012: "<a   href="Estonian_Health_Worker_Strike_(2012).md" class="wikilink"
  title="Estonian Health Worker Strike">"Estonian Health Worker Strike</a>
- 2015:
  "<a   href="Dita_(Factory).md" class="wikilink" title="Dita factory">"Dita
  factory</a> takeover (Bosnia and Herzegovina)
