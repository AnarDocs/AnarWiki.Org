The **Spanish Civil War** took place between
"<a   href="Timeline_of_Anarchism_in_Southern_Europe.md" class="wikilink"
title="1936 and 1939">"1936 and 1939</a> between the
"<a   href="Republicans_(Spanish_Civil_War).md" class="wikilink"
title="Republicans">"Republicans</a> and
"<a   href="Nationalists_(Spanish_Civil_War).md" class="wikilink"
title="Nationalists">"Nationalists</a> for control of the
"<a   href="Spain.md" class="wikilink" title="Spanish state">"Spanish state</a>,
ending in a Nationalist victory. The Nationalists were composed of
right-leaning capitalists, the church, the army, fascists, monarchists,
landowners and much of the middle class. The Republicans were composed
of much of the working class,
"<a   href="Peasants.md" class="wikilink" title="peasantry">"peasantry</a>, the
navy, left-leaning liberals, socialists, communists and
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchists">"anarchists</a>.
The Republicans notably begin to splinter into their own civil war owing
to disputes between the anarchists and pro-Stalin communists, one of
many factors leading to a Nationalist victory. Between 150,000 and
2,000,000 people were killed in the war.
