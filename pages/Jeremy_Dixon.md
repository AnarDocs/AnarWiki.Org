**Jeremy Dixon** is an
<a href="anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchist">anarchist</a> and
lawyer from Naarm/Melbourne, Australia.

In 1991, he was arrested by police in Melbourne during protests against
the Gulf War.

He has written the short piece *What is Anarchism? What is
Anarcho-Syndicalism?* which can be read here:
<http://www.spunk.org/texts/intro/sp001530.html>
