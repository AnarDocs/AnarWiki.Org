The **Russian Federation** is an authoritarian
<a href="Capitalism" class="wikilink" title="capitalist">capitalist</a>
<a href="List_of_States" class="wikilink" title="state">state</a>
located in Northern Asia and Eastern Europe, bordering North Korea,
<a href="China" class="wikilink" title="China">China</a>, Mongolia,
Kazakhstan, Azerbaijan, Georgia, Ukraine, Belarus, Lithuania, Latvia,
Estonia, Finland and Poland.
