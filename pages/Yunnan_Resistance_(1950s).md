The **Yunnan Resistance** was a small
"<a   href="Guerilla_Warfare.md" class="wikilink"
title="guerilla war">"guerilla war</a> waged by
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchists">"anarchists</a>
in "<a   href="China.md" class="wikilink" title="Southern China">"Southern
China</a> near the border of
"<a   href="Myanmar.md" class="wikilink" title="Myanmar">"Myanmar</a> and
"<a   href="Vietnam.md" class="wikilink" title="Vietnam">"Vietnam</a>. Most of
the guerillas also fought against the Japanese and Nationalists during
the invasion of China,
"<a   href="Timeline_of_Anarchism_in_Eastern_Asia.md" class="wikilink"
title="during the 1950s">"during the 1950s</a>, it was one of the few
acts of resistance to the new
"<a   href="Authoritarian_Socialism.md" class="wikilink"
title="authoritarian socialist">"authoritarian socialist</a> government.
It was led by
"<a   href="Chu_Cha-pei.md" class="wikilink" title="Chu Cha-pei">"Chu
Cha-pei</a>, who was inspired by
"<a   href="Nestor_Makhno.md" class="wikilink" title="Nestor Makhno">"Nestor
Makhno</a> and the
"<a   href="Black_Army_(Ukraine).md" class="wikilink" title="Black Army">"Black
Army</a>.
