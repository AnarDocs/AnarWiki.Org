The **Yunnan Resistance** was a small
<a href="Guerilla_Warfare" class="wikilink"
title="guerilla war">guerilla war</a> waged by
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchists">anarchists</a>
in <a href="China" class="wikilink" title="Southern China">Southern
China</a> near the border of
<a href="Myanmar" class="wikilink" title="Myanmar">Myanmar</a> and
<a href="Vietnam" class="wikilink" title="Vietnam">Vietnam</a>. Most of
the guerillas also fought against the Japanese and Nationalists during
the invasion of China,
<a href="Timeline_of_Anarchism_in_Eastern_Asia" class="wikilink"
title="during the 1950s">during the 1950s</a>, it was one of the few
acts of resistance to the new
<a href="Authoritarian_Socialism" class="wikilink"
title="authoritarian socialist">authoritarian socialist</a> government.
It was led by
<a href="Chu_Cha-pei" class="wikilink" title="Chu Cha-pei">Chu
Cha-pei</a>, who was inspired by
<a href="Nestor_Makhno" class="wikilink" title="Nestor Makhno">Nestor
Makhno</a> and the
<a href="Black_Army_(Ukraine)" class="wikilink" title="Black Army">Black
Army</a>.
