Alper Sapan was a pacifist anarchist who was killed in the
"<a   href="Suruç_Massacre.md" class="wikilink" title="Suruç Massacre">Suruç
Massacre</a> on 20 July 2015. At the time they were going to
"<a   href="Rojava.md" class="wikilink" title="Rojava">Rojava</a> to help the
children.

<figure>
<img src="Alper-sapan.jpg" title="Alper-sapan.jpg" />
<figcaption>Alper-sapan.jpg</figcaption>
</figure>

> “Hello, I am Alper Sapan. I am an 19-year-old anarchist. I am against
> the injustice of state, exploitation and oppression. I am rejecting
> murder of people, violence and state. I won’t die for anybody, I won’t
> kill for anybody and I won’t be anybody’s soldier. For the world where
> there is no war, no nation, no border; for freedom, I am listening to
> my heart and rejecting the military service. Let’s destroy militarism
> before militarism destroys all of us.”
