a variety of reasons that involve withdrawing labor from a workplace.

### Green Ban

*Main Article:
<a href="Green_Bans" class="wikilink" title="Green Bans">Green Bans</a>*

Green Bans refers to strikes undertaken by construction workers where
they refuse to work on projects that hurt the environment, low-income
communities or sites of cultural heritage. They were mainly undertaken
in <a href="Australia" class="wikilink" title="Australia">Australia</a>
in the 1970s by the
<a href="Builders_Laborers_Federation" class="wikilink"
title="Builders Laborers Federation">Builders Laborers Federation</a>.
