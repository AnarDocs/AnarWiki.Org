The **Red Square Protests of 1968** was an
"<a   href="Peace_Movements.md" class="wikilink"
title="anti-war protest">"anti-war protest</a> in Moscow in
"<a   href="Timeline_of_Anarchism_in_Eastern_Europe.md" class="wikilink"
title="1968">"1968</a> against the USSR's invasion of
"<a   href="Socialist_Czechoslovakia.md" class="wikilink"
title="Czechoslovakia">"Czechoslovakia</a> in order to crush the
"<a   href="Prague_Spring.md" class="wikilink" title="Prague Spring">"Prague
Spring</a>. It was part of a
"<a   href="Revolutions_of_1967_-_1975.md" class="wikilink"
title="broader global wave of unrest">"broader global wave of unrest</a>
and was one of the only public protests in the
"<a   href="USSR.md" class="wikilink" title="USSR">"USSR</a>.
