The **Workers' Solidarity Alliance** (**WSA**) is an
"<a   href="Anarcho-Syndicalism.md" class="wikilink"
title="anarcho-syndicalist">anarcho-syndicalist</a>
"<a   href="List_of_Anarchist_Organizations.md" class="wikilink"
title="organization">organization</a> based in the
"<a   href="United_States_of_America.md" class="wikilink" title="USA">USA</a>.
It is member of the
"<a   href="International_Workers&#39;_Association.md" class="wikilink"
title="International Workers&#39; Association">International Workers'
Association</a>. Founded in
"<a   href="Timeline_of_Anarchism_in_North_America.md" class="wikilink"
title="1984">1984</a>[^1], it can be contacted
[here](https://workersolidarity.org/contact-wsa/).
