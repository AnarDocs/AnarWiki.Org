**A People's History of Australia since 1788** is a 1988
<a href="List_of_Anarchist_Books.md" class="wikilink" title="book">book</a>
by Verity Burgmann and Jenny Lee that covers various areas in the
<a href="history_of_Australia.md" class="wikilink"
title="history of Australia">history of Australia</a> that usually
aren't discussed such as the development of
<a href="capitalism.md" class="wikilink" title="capitalism">capitalism</a>
in <a href="Australia.md" class="wikilink" title="Australia">Australia</a>,
the resistance to the establishment of
<a href="Private_Property.md" class="wikilink"
title="private property">private property</a> by
<a href="Australian_Aboriginals.md" class="wikilink"
title="Australian Aboriginals">Australian Aboriginals</a>, the lives of
convicts and immigrants, Australia's
<a href="Slavery.md" class="wikilink" title="slave trade">slave trade</a>
and efforts to establish a
<a href="Imperialism.md" class="wikilink" title="pacific empire">pacific
empire</a>.
