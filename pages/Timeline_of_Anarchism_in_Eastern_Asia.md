![](EastAsia.JPG "EastAsia.JPG") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in Eastern Asia (a region that includes the
"<a   href="List_of_States.md" class="wikilink" title="states">"states</a> of
"<a   href="China.md" class="wikilink" title="China">"China</a>,
"<a   href="Japan.md" class="wikilink" title="Japan">"Japan</a>,

"<a   href="North_Korea.md" class="wikilink" title="North Korea">"North

"<a   href="Hong_Kong.md" class="wikilink" title="Hong Kong">"Hong Kong</a> and
"<a   href="Macau.md" class="wikilink" title="Macau">"Macau</a>) that are
relevant to
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">"anarchism</a>.
