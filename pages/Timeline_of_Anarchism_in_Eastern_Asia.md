![](EastAsia.JPG "EastAsia.JPG") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in Eastern Asia (a region that includes the
<a href="List_of_States" class="wikilink" title="states">states</a> of
<a href="China" class="wikilink" title="China">China</a>,
<a href="Japan" class="wikilink" title="Japan">Japan</a>,
<a href="South_Korea" class="wikilink" title="South Korea">South
Korea</a>,
<a href="North_Korea" class="wikilink" title="North Korea">North
Korea</a>, <a href="Taiwan" class="wikilink" title="Taiwan">Taiwan</a>,
<a href="Hong_Kong" class="wikilink" title="Hong Kong">Hong Kong</a> and
<a href="Macau" class="wikilink" title="Macau">Macau</a>) that are
relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.
