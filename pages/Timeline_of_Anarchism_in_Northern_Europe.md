![](NorthernEruope.png "NorthernEruope.png") This is a timeline of
various political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental
events that have occurred in Northern Europe (An area comprising the
states of the
<a href="United_Kingdom" class="wikilink" title="UK">UK</a>,
<a href="Ireland" class="wikilink" title="Ireland">Ireland</a>,
<a href="Iceland" class="wikilink" title="Iceland">Iceland</a>,
<a href="Norway" class="wikilink" title="Norway">Norway</a>,
<a href="Sweden" class="wikilink" title="Sweden">Sweden</a>,
<a href="Finland" class="wikilink" title="Finland">Finland</a> and
<a href="Denmark" class="wikilink" title="Denmark">Denmark</a>) that are
relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.

- 1910:
  <a href="Central_Organisation_of_the_Workers_of_Sweden" class="wikilink"
  title="Central Organisation of the Workers of Sweden">Central
  Organisation of the Workers of Sweden</a> founded.
- 1946:
  <a href="43_Group" class="wikilink" title="43 Group">43 Group</a>
  founded (UK)
- 1962:
  <a href="62_Group" class="wikilink" title="62 Group">62 Group</a>
  founded (UK)
- 1971: <a href="Freetown_Christiania" class="wikilink"
  title="Freetown Christiania">Freetown Christiania</a>founded (Denmark)
- 1979: <a href="Dublin_McDonald&#39;s_Strike_(1979)" class="wikilink"
  title="Dublin McDonald&#39;s Strike">Dublin McDonald's Strike</a>
  (Ireland)
- 1982: <a href="Blitz_House" class="wikilink" title="Blitz House">Blitz
  House</a> founded (Norway)
- 1998: <a href="Danish_General_Strike_(1998)" class="wikilink"
  title="Danish General Strike">Danish General Strike</a>
- 1999:
  <a href="Björn_Söderberg" class="wikilink" title="Björn Söderberg">Björn
  Söderberg</a> murdered (Sweden)
- 2019:
  <a href="TEFL_Workers&#39;_Union" class="wikilink" title="TEFL">TEFL</a>
