![](SoutheastAsia.JPG "SoutheastAsia.JPG") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in Southeast Asia (Indonesia, Vietnam, Thailand,
Myanmar, Philippines, Laos, Cambodia, Singapore, Malaysia, Timor-Leste,
Brunei) that are relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.

- 1945: <a href="Indonesian_National_Revolution" class="wikilink"
  title="Indonesian National Revolution">Indonesian National
  Revolution</a>
- 1945:
  <a href="Saigon_Commune" class="wikilink" title="Saigon Commune">Saigon
  Commune</a> (Vietnam)
- 1975: <a href="Civil_Rights_Movement_(Thailand)" class="wikilink"
  title="Civil Rights Movement">Civil Rights Movement</a> (Thailand)
- 2007:
  <a href="PT_Istana" class="wikilink" title="PT Istana">PT Istana</a>
  takeover (Indonesia)
