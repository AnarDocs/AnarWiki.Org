The **Autonomous Workers' Union** (APC) is an
<a href="Anarcho-Syndicalism" class="wikilink"
title="anarcho-syndicalist">anarcho-syndicalist</a> organisation based
in <a href="Bulgaria" class="wikilink" title="Bulgaria">Bulgaria</a>.
This group frequently acts in solidarity with workers' from
<a href="Turkey" class="wikilink" title="Turkey">Turkey</a>,
<a href="Ukraine" class="wikilink" title="Ukraine">Ukraine</a> and
<a href="Russia" class="wikilink" title="Russia">Russia</a>, as well as
Bulgarian immigrant workers in other countries. On the 8th of March,
2015, the APC
<a href="Picketing" class="wikilink" title="picketed">picketed</a> a
nightclub in Varna that had not paid its workers.[^1] On the 5th of
September, 2017, 150 members of the APC protested outside the Bulgarian
parliament in Sofia, calling for harsher action on wage theft.[^2]
