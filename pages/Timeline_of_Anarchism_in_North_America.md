![](NorthAmerica.jpg "NorthAmerica.jpg") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in South America (a region encompassing the
"<a   href="List_of_States.md" class="wikilink" title="states">states</a> of
"<a   href="Canada.md" class="wikilink" title="Canada">Canada</a>,
"<a   href="Greenland.md" class="wikilink" title="Greenland">Greenland</a>,
"<a   href="United_States_of_America.md" class="wikilink" title="USA">USA</a>
and "<a   href="Mexico.md" class="wikilink" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>) that
are relevant to
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.
