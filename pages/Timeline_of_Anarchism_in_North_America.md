![](NorthAmerica.jpg "NorthAmerica.jpg") This is a timeline of various
political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental events
that have occurred in South America (a region encompassing the
<a href="List_of_States" class="wikilink" title="states">states</a> of
<a href="Canada" class="wikilink" title="Canada">Canada</a>,
<a href="Greenland" class="wikilink" title="Greenland">Greenland</a>,
<a href="United_States_of_America" class="wikilink" title="USA">USA</a>
and <a href="Mexico" class="wikilink" title="Mexico">Mexico</a>) that
are relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.

## 1100s

- 1100s: <a href="Hopi" class="wikilink" title="Hopi">Hopi</a> villages
  are established.
- 1142: <a href="Haudenosaunee_Confederacy" class="wikilink"
  title="Haudenosaunee Confederacy">Haudenosaunee Confederacy</a>
  created.

  title="Canadian General Strike">Canadian General Strike</a>

  title="BC Telephone Work-In">BC Telephone Work-In</a> (Canada)
