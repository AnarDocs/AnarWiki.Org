The **Revolutions of 1848 - 1871** were a
<a href="Revolutionary_Wave.md" class="wikilink"
title="revolutionary wave">revolutionary wave</a> consisting off mass
political unrest and revolts around the world in the mid-19th century.
The uprisings were mainly of a
<a href="Liberalism.md" class="wikilink" title="liberal">liberal</a>
<a href="Capitalism.md" class="wikilink" title="capitalist">capitalist</a>
and anti-colonial nature, and successfully spelt the death knells of
<a href="feudalism.md" class="wikilink" title="feudalism">feudalism</a>.

    title="Denmark">Denmark</a>
- 1848: Matale Rebellion (Sri Lanka)

- 1854: Eureka Rebellion (Australia)

- 1859: Second Italian War of Independence
