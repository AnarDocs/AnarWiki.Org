**Workers' councils** are organisations that govern industry or a
municaplity made up of temporary and instantly revocable
"<a   href="delegates.md" class="wikilink" title="delegates">delegates</a>
elected in the region's workplaces. A variation is a **soldiers'
council**, when the delegates are chosen amongst
("<a   href="Mutiny.md" class="wikilink" title="mutinous">mutinous</a>)
soldiers. A mix of workers and soldiers have also existed

## Timeline of Workers' and Soldiers' Councils

- 1871: The
  "<a   href="Paris_Commune.md" class="wikilink" title="Paris Commune">Paris
  Commune</a> in
  "<a   href="France.md" class="wikilink" title="France">France</a> is seen by
  some as a workers' council.
- 1917: Approximately 13 workers' councils are
  "<a   href="Norwegian_Workers&#39;_Council_Wave.md" class="wikilink"
  title="set up">set up</a> across cities and towns in the
  "<a   href="United_Kingdom.md" class="wikilink" title="UK">UK</a>.
- 1918: Approximately 91 workers' councils are
  "<a   href="Norwegian_Workers&#39;_Council_Wave.md" class="wikilink"
  title="set up">set up</a> across cities and towns in
  "<a   href="Norway.md" class="wikilink" title="Norway">Norway</a>.
- 1919: 2,500 soldiers in Kantara, Egypt form a
  "<a   href="Kantara_Soldiers&#39;_Council.md" class="wikilink"
  title="soldiers&#39; council">soldiers' council</a> during a mutiny.
- 1919: Approximately 7 workers' councils are set up across cities in
  the
  "<a   href="United_States_of_America.md" class="wikilink" title="USA">USA</a>.
- 1945: Tramworkers' form a workers' council during the
  "<a   href="Saigon_Commune.md" class="wikilink" title="Saigon Uprising">Saigon
  Uprising</a>.
- 1968: Workers' councils form across France during the May Events.
- 1973: Workers' councils begin forming in Chile.
- 1978: Workers' councils begin forming in Iran during the Iranian
  Revolution.
