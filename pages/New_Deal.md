The **New Deal** was a series of programs, reforms and regulations
enacted by the
"<a   href="United_States_of_America.md" class="wikilink" title="US">US</a>
government from 1933 to 1939 to help stop the
"<a   href="Great_Depression.md" class="wikilink"
title="Great Depression">Great Depression</a>. Many have argued this
period was the closest the US came to socialism, but others have argued
(including the president at the time,
"<a   href="Franklin_Delano_Roosevelt.md" class="wikilink" title="FDR">FDR</a>)
that it prevented communist revolution developing in the US.

## Good

- Creation of the
  "<a   href="Civilian_Conservation_Corps_(USA).md" class="wikilink"
  title="Civilian Conservation Corps">Civilian Conservation Corps</a>
  that helped provide employment and restore ecosystems.
- Creation of a system of mortgages to help poorer people rich the
  middle class by building wealth through home ownership.

## Bad

- Prevent a revolution against capitalism.
- Stealing of indigenous land.
- Creation of redlining, ensuring African-Americans could not access
  home ownership easily, preventing wealth being built in black
  communities.
