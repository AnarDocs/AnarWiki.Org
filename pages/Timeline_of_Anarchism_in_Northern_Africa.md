![](NorthernAfrica.JPG "NorthernAfrica.JPG") This is a timeline of
various political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental
events that have occurred in Northern Africa (compromising the
<a href="List_of_States" class="wikilink" title="states">states</a> of
<a href="Egypt" class="wikilink" title="Egypt">Egypt</a>,
<a href="Sudan" class="wikilink" title="Sudan">Sudan</a>,
<a href="Libya" class="wikilink" title="Libya">Libya</a>,
<a href="Tunisia" class="wikilink" title="Tunisia">Tunisia</a>,
<a href="Algeria" class="wikilink" title="Algeria">Algeria</a>,
<a href="Morocco" class="wikilink" title="Morocco">Morocco</a> and
<a href="Western_Sahara" class="wikilink" title="Western Sahara">Western
Sahara</a>) that are relevant to
<a href="Anarchism" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">anarchism</a>.

- 1954: Algerian Revolution

- 1962: Algerians successfully prevent the break out of a civil war.
- 1962: Algerian autogestion.
- 1977: Egyptian Bread Riots
- 1985: Numeiri dictatorship collapses in Sudan
- 1990: <a href="Moroccan_General_Strike_(1990)" class="wikilink"
