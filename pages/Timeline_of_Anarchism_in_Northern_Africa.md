various political, legal, military, social, economic and environmental
events that have occurred in Northern Africa (compromising the
"<a   href="List_of_States.md" class="wikilink" title="states">"states</a> of
"<a   href="Egypt.md" class="wikilink" title="Egypt">"Egypt</a>,
"<a   href="Sudan.md" class="wikilink" title="Sudan">"Sudan</a>,

"<a   href="Tunisia.md" class="wikilink" title="Tunisia">"Tunisia</a>,
"<a   href="Algeria.md" class="wikilink" title="Algeria">"Algeria</a>,
"<a   href="Morocco.md" class="wikilink" title="Morocco">"Morocco</a> and
"<a   href="Western_Sahara.md" class="wikilink" title="Western Sahara">"Western
Sahara</a>) that are relevant to
"<a   href="Anarchism.md" class="wikilink" title="anarchism">"anarchism</a>.
